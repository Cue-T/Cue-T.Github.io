About the Artist

I have been drawing/doodling for as long as I can remember, but in elementary/primary school is when I realized my gift. I fully embraced my gift, practiced every chance I got, learned new skills, and improved my artistic skills. Then I saw digital art, and for the longest, I wanted to learn.

Around mid-2021, while I was attending my massage therapy class, we were doing a group project which needed my skills. When my classmates saw what I made, they encouraged me to attend art school after graduating from massage therapy. Well, I looked into a few schools, and I decided to get into Graphic Design at the Florida Institute of Recording, Sound, and Technology (FIRST Institute) in Orlando. Getting into the course, I only wanted the primary knowledge of Adobe Creative Pak, but as I graduate in May 2022, I've gained another passion.
